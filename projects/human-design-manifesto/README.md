# The Emotional Manifesto: Interactive Human Design Guide

An immersive, interactive web experience for a **5/1 Manifesting Generator with Emotional Authority**. This digital manifesto combines Human Design wisdom with modern web technology to create a personalized guide for understanding and living your unique energetic blueprint.

## Features

- **50 Daily Affirmations**: Personalized to your specific Human Design
- **Interactive Energy Centers**: Click to learn about Sacral, Throat, Root, and Solar Plexus
- **Clickable Gates**: Explore Gates 64, 63, 9, and 16 in depth
- **Animated Emotional Wave**: Visual representation of emotional authority
- **Sacred Strategy**: The Respond → Feel → Decide process
- **5/1 Profile Guide**: The Heretic and Investigator
- **Print-Friendly**: Download or save as PDF

## Quick Start

Simply open `index.html` in your web browser and begin your journey of self-discovery!

## Using Your Manifesto

### Daily Practice
1. **Morning Ritual**: Read your daily affirmation
2. **Decision-Making**: Reference emotional authority guidance
3. **Alignment Check**: Review Not-Self vs. Signature themes

### Interactive Features
- Click energy center cards for detailed information
- Click gate cards to explore their wisdom
- Generate new affirmations with the button
- Print/save as PDF for offline reference

## What is a 5/1 Manifesting Generator?

- **Manifesting Generator**: Multi-passionate beings who respond to life then move quickly
- **Emotional Authority**: Must ride emotional waves before making decisions
- **5/1 Profile**: Heretic (Line 5) who provides solutions + Investigator (Line 1) who needs foundations

## Customization

Edit `affirmations` array in `script.js` to add your own affirmations, or modify `centerInfo` and `gateInfo` objects to personalize for different Human Design types.
